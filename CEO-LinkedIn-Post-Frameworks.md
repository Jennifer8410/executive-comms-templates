# CEO LinkedIn Post Frameworks (Copy/Paste)

## Framework 1: POV + Lesson (high engagement)
**Hook:** A contrarian truth or strong POV about **[industry/topic]**.  
**Story:** What you saw / learned in **[real moment]**.  
**Lesson:** The takeaway in 1–2 lines.  
**Apply:** What you/your company is doing about it.  
**Close:** Ask a simple question.

**Example fill:**
- “Most companies get **[X]** wrong…”
- “Here’s what changed my mind: **[moment]**…”
- “The real lesson: **[lesson]**…”
- “We’re applying this by **[action]**…”
- “How are you handling **[question]**?”

---

## Framework 2: Announcement (clean + credible)
**Headline:** We’re launching/partnering/hiring **[thing]**.  
**Why:** The problem it solves.  
**What:** 2–3 bullets of what’s included.  
**Thank you:** credit partners/team.  
**CTA:** link or “DM me if…”

---

## Framework 3: Customer story (trust builder)
**Who:** a customer/team member (no confidential info).  
**Problem:** what they faced.  
**What changed:** what you delivered.  
**Result:** measurable or emotional outcome.  
**Close:** what you’re focused on next.

---

## Tone toggles (pick one)
- **Direct:** short sentences, fewer adjectives.
- **Warm:** add gratitude + people-first language.
- **Visionary:** zoom out to mission and future impact.
