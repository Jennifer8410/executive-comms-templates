## INTERNAL VERSION (employees)

### Subject line options
- [Action/Update] [Announcement] + what it means
- [Update] [Short headline] — next steps

### Opening (what’s happening, in 1–2 sentences)
Today we’re sharing that **[announcement]**.

### Why this matters (context)
This matters because **[why now / strategic reason / benefit]**.

### What’s changing (bullet list)
- **What changes:**  
- **What stays the same:**  
- **Timing:**  
- **Who is impacted:**  

### What to do next (clear actions)
- Employees should: **[do X by date]**
- Managers should: **[do Y by date]**
- Where to direct questions: **[email/slack/channel]**

### Human close
Thank you for **[effort/support]**. We’ll share the next update **[when]**.
